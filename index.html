-------- What Is Safeguarding? --------

In the real world, safeguarding refers to the policies and actions that protect people’s health, well-being, and human rights. Its purpose is to ensure that individuals — especially children and vulnerable adults — are kept safe from harm, abuse, neglect, and exploitation.

Effective safeguarding involves clear communication, risk assessment, safe working practices, proper training, and knowing how to report concerns. Any organisation that works with the public has a responsibility to follow safeguarding guidelines and create a safe and supportive environment.

While this website uses a fictional theme about guarding safes, this section is included to demonstrate an understanding of genuine safeguarding principles as required by the project.
